What do I learn for Diploma in Library Informatics?

In the first semester, I learn about the introduction to information. The subjects
includes; Introduction to Information Skills, Introduction to Information
Management, Information and Communication Technology Application, and
Fundamentals of Management.

For the second semester, I was introduced to databases. The assignments for the
subject were pretty hectic but we managed to get through it and passed. We also
learned about communication. I remember we were given a task to public speaking
in front of the whole class. We learned about Organization and Access to
Information, Introduction to Library Management, Fundamental of Data Management,
and Communication Skills for Information Professionals.

The third semester has taught us the skill of coding. It was the first time for me to
code. Same as last semester, it was hard for me to understand and learn how to
code. Luckily, I managed to pass the subject. For this semester, the subject
includes Digital Preservation in Library Environment, Information Security for
Libraries, Programming for Libraries, and Descriptive Cataloging.

For the current semester I am having, semester four, I am learning about Adobe
Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Animator. Other subjects include
Metadata Development in Information Environment, Introduction to Web Content
Development, Subject Cataloging and Classification, and Multimedia and Digital
Publishing in Libraries.
