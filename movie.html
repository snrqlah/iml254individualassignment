The Book of Life

This is one of my favourite childhood movies. I could never get bored of it. The movie
starts when two gods; Xibalba and La Muerte make a wager on 3 kids, Manolo, Maria,
and Joaquin. They made a bet that would marry Maria. Xibalba chooses Joaquin and
La Muerte chooses Manolo. They made the wager because Xibalba wanted to control
La Muerte's land. So, they decided whoever the winner would control both the Land of
the Rememberers, which is La Muerte's land and the Land of the Forgotten, which is
Xibalba's land. Next is a quick brief of the characters. Manolo Sanchez is a bullfighter
and a guitarist who would express his feelings through music. Maria Posada is the
general's daughter and known as the Jewel of the Town. Joaquin Mondragon is the
town hero who has the Medal of Everlasting Life given by Xibalba to win the bet.
I love this movie because it teaches us to be true to ourselves. We can make oursleves
happy with the things we love, rather than the things we are forced to love.
